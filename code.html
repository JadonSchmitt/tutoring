About Me

Hey there, I’m Jadon!

I'm an experienced math and test prep tutor based in Lake Oswego/Southwest Portland.

I tutor a variety of subjects, including AP Calculus and AP Statistics.

I have 4+ years of experience at the following institutions:

  • North Avenue Education: I primarily tutored SAT, ACT, AP Calc, AP Stats, and trig.
    This is perhaps the most prestigious Portland-based tutoring company, charging up to $200/hour.
    I’m committed to offering that expert service at a fraction of the price!
  • AmeriCorps: I taught Algebra 1 at Tigard High School.
  • University of Oregon: I tutored student athletes in college algebra and economics.
  • Minds Matter: I currently volunteer as an algebra and geometry tutor at this nonprofit.

I graduated with a 4.0 GPA from the University of Oregon with degrees in mathematics and economics. Aside from tutoring, I've worked as a technology consultant at Deloitte and a research assistant at the National Bureau of Economic Research. This fall, I’ll start my PhD in Economics.

Here are a few of the tenets of my tutoring philosophy:

  • Personal connection: Warm, patient rapport makes learning less intimidating and more engaging.
  • More than memorization: I can help explain why algorithms/formulas work and how they can be used to solve real-world problems.
  • Everyone can learn anything: If a student is confused, it’s my responsibility to explain the concept in a different way until it clicks!
